Manage Your FuelFlux Account or Data

Welcome to the official FuelFlux account and data management page. We value your privacy and give you the option to either delete your entire account or remove specific data while keeping your account active. Below are the steps for both options:

Option 1: Delete Your Entire Account

If you wish to delete your entire FuelFlux account, follow the steps below:

  • Send an email to support@fuelflux.in with the subject line "Delete My FuelFlux Account".
  • In the email, provide the following information:
    • Your full name
    • Your registered email address
    • Any additional details that can help us identify your account

Once we receive your request, we will:

  • Permanently delete all of your personal data (including your name, phone number, vehicle number, and email address) within 24 hours of your request.
  • No data will be retained, and nothing will be shared with any third parties.

Option 2: Delete Specific Data (Keep Account)

If you'd prefer to keep your account but delete specific data (e.g., phone number, vehicle details), follow these steps:

  • Send an email to support@fuelflux.in with the subject line "Delete Specific Data in My FuelFlux Account".
  • In the email, specify the data you wish to delete, such as:
    • Phone Number
    • Vehicle Number
    • Email Address

We will remove the specified data within 24 hours while ensuring that your account remains active. You can continue using FuelFlux with the remaining data.

Data Retention Policy

We ensure that:

  • Your data is deleted within 24 hours of your request, whether you choose to delete your entire account or just specific data.
  • No data will be retained beyond what is necessary for the operation of your account or as required by law.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding account deletion or data handling, feel free to reach out to us at support@fuelflux.in.
